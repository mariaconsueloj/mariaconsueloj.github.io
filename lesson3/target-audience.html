Target Audience

The target audience for this website is for people tht like to do outdoor activities and people that live around those areas, so they can plan ahead the clothes, activities or other things that they want to do or places to go.

Scenarios

What is the weather in the areas around (specific place)?

The visitors that are going to travel should know how the weather is in the areas around where they want to go to see at what time they should leave or if they can go to do the activity that they want to. They heard that is going to snow in some of the areas around the place they want to go, and they don't want to go if it's going to snow or rain there.
